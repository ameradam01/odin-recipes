Chicken fried rice

Description

Chicken fried rice is a savory and satisfying dish that combines tender pieces of chicken, fluffy rice, and a medley of vegetables, all stir-fried to perfection. This classic Asian-inspired dish is a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. Succulent chicken is cooked to a golden brown, imparting a deliciously savory taste. The rice, stir-fried with aromatic seasonings and sauces, becomes infused with the flavors of the dish. Mixed vegetables, such as carrots, peas, and onions, add vibrant colors and a delightful crunch. Each mouthful of chicken fried rice offers a delightful combination of tender protein, fragrant rice, and a symphony of flavors that make it a popular and comforting choice for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups cooked white rice, cold
  • 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Preparing the dish

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat egg and water together in small bowl. Melt butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-low heat; add egg and cook without stirring, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds; set aside.
  3. Heat oil in the same skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes. Stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce, and pepper. Cook and stir for about 5 minutes; stir in shredded egg. Serve hot.
