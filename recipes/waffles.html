Chocolate Waffles


Description

Chocolate waffles are a delectable treat that combines the irresistible flavors of chocolate and the delightful texture of a crispy yet fluffy waffle. These mouthwatering delights are made by incorporating rich cocoa powder or melted chocolate into the waffle batter, resulting in a decadent and indulgent taste. With each bite, the warm and fragrant chocolate permeates the air, tantalizing the senses. The waffles boast a golden brown exterior that provides a satisfying crunch, while the inside remains soft and light. Whether enjoyed as a breakfast indulgence or as a delightful dessert, chocolate waffles are a delightful fusion of flavors and textures that are sure to satisfy any chocolate lover's cravings.

Ingredients

Waffles:
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • nonstick cooking spray
Hard Sauce:
  • 1 tablespoon butter, softened
  • ¾ cup confectioners' sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Preparing the dish

  1. Preheat a waffle iron.
  2. Make waffles: Stir together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and salt in a large mixing bowl. Stir in milk, eggs, and butter until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Make hard sauce: stir together softened butter, confectioners' sugar, vanilla extract, and milk. Sauce should be fairly stiff. Set aside.
  4. Spray preheated waffle iron with nonstick cooking spray. Pour waffle mix onto hot waffle iron. Cook until golden brown. Serve hot with hard sauce.
